I'm a pharmacist and clinical infusion pharmacy manager with a background in retail and infusion care. Much of my work involves helping patients understand complex therapies, which led me to start building Dosecraft — an interactive simulation that uses game mechanics to teach how medications work.

This site documents that process. It's a place to share projects, experiments, and lessons learned while teaching myself software development and exploring ideas at the intersection of healthcare, education, and technology.
